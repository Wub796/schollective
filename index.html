How it works

From sign-up to
meaningful dialogue

We replaced academic cold-email culture with a system designed for clarity, safety, and genuine connection.

1

Create your profile

Students sign up instantly. Professors apply and are manually verified using their institutional email address.

2

Discover the right mentor

Browse professors by academic field, research area, and current availability. Find someone who works in your domain.

3

Submit a structured request

Craft a focused mentorship request — not a cold email. The structure helps professors respond efficiently and meaningfully.

4

Engage in guided dialogue

Communicate through organized one-on-one threads. Real academic guidance, on your terms.

Platform features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Lightweight, focused, and free of the noise that plagues generic social platforms.

🔐
Role-locked accounts

Roles are permanently assigned at onboarding — no confusion, no switching. The system always knows who you are.

📬
Structured messaging

No unstructured spam. All communication happens through organized one-on-one threads tied to a specific request.

✅
Admin verification

Every professor is manually approved using their institutional email — students always know who they're talking to.

📊
Unified dashboard

Track all requests, active conversations, and responses in one clean hub — tailored to your role.

🔎
Professor discovery

Browse and filter professors by field of expertise, institution, and current availability.

🛡️
Safe, controlled access

Same-role messaging is blocked by design. Professors decide whether to engage with each request.
